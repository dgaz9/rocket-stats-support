About

RocketStats AI is a private, invite-only application for softball teams. It provides ESPN-quality analytics including:

  • Batting, fielding, and pitching statistics
  • Player comparisons and career tracking
  • Opponent scouting with head-to-head breakdowns
  • Shareable trading-card style stat cards
  • AI chat assistant that knows your team\'s data
  • Multi-season support with GameChanger data integration

Access requires an invite code from your team administrator.

FAQ

How do I get access?

You need an invite code from your team coach or administrator. Enter it during registration to create your account.

Which teams are supported?

RocketStats AI currently supports teams whose data has been imported by an administrator. Contact your team admin to get started.

Where does the data come from?

Statistics are imported from GameChanger and can also be entered manually via CSV import. Data is synced automatically.

Is the AI chat accurate?

The AI assistant uses your team\'s actual data from the database. It can look up stats, compare players, and answer questions about games and seasons.

Can I use this on iPad?

Yes! RocketStats AI supports iPhone, iPad, and is optimized for all screen sizes.
